Our purpose is to assist restaurant owners, general managers and kitchen managers with their kitchen maintenance and repairs. We help keep your commercial kitchen operating efficiently and keep your kitchen up to code with the health inspectors.

  • The Risks of Worn Out or Cracked Gaskets

    • They no longer create an airtight seal causing for food spoilage.
    • It can lead to excessive energy costs.
    • It can make your compressor motors run overtime.
    • Health inspector will flag, assess fines or other penalties.
    • It can cause immediate expenses.

  • Do I Need New Gaskets?

    Here are some key indications that it may be time for a new gasket on your equipment:

    • A gasket becomes worn out or torn.
    • A poor seal: gaskets rely on a magnetic seal which can show no visible signs of wear yet still not properly seal your cooler or freezer.
